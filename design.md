# Design Document

## Screens

### Profile configuration

#### Simple

- Weight
- Height
- Gender
- Age
- Activity level (coarse)
  - Sedentary (little or no exercise)
  - Lightly active (1-3 times a week)
  - Moderately active (4-5 times a week)
  - Active (daily exercise or intense exercise 3-4 times a week)
  - Very active (intense exercise 6-7 times a week)
  - Extremely active (very intense exercise daily or physical job)
- Goal
  - Lose weight fast (-1 pound/week)
  - Lose weight (-.5 pounds/week)
  - Maintain weight
  - Gain weight (+.5 pounds/week)
  - Gain weight fast (+1 pound/week)
- Allergies/Dietary restrictions

#### Advanced

- Macronutrient ratio
- Activity level (fine)
- Kitchen equipment
